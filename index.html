Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 07, 2025

Summary

The Cancelled Chrome Extension does not collect personal information, browsing history, or usage analytics. All operations, including ESG score matching and badge display, occur locally on your device. We value your privacy and have designed the extension to minimize data access and maximize transparency.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Personal Data

Our extension does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information. The ESG score lookup is performed locally within your browser and does not involve sending any personal data or browsing history to external servers.

Usage Data

We do not track or store your browsing history. The extension temporarily analyzes the current active tab’s content to identify the company name for score matching, but this is done locally and not logged or shared.

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

The Cancelled Chrome Extension does not use cookies, web beacons, tracking scripts, or similar technologies. No data is stored beyond what is necessary to enable functionality such as ESG badge display and score matching, and that data remains on your device.

Chrome Extension Permissions

To operate effectively, the Cancelled extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab – to access the content of the current tab when activated.
  • tabs – to identify which domain or company the user is currently visiting.
  • storage – to store temporary local data such as the last ESG score retrieved.
  • scripting – to dynamically run content scripts required for company name detection.

These permissions are used solely for the ESG-matching functionality and never for tracking or profiling users.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us. If We become aware that We have collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, We take steps to remove that information from Our servers.

Chrome Web Store User Data Policy Compliance

The Cancelled Chrome Extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:

  • We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information or browsing history.
  • We only use tab URL and minimal page content to provide ESG score functionality, which is the single stated purpose of the extension.
  • No user data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.
  • No human has access to any user data; all functionality is processed locally in the browser.
  • We do not use or transfer user data for personalized advertising or marketing purposes.
  • The extension does not determine credit-worthiness or perform any financial profiling.

The use of any information adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
